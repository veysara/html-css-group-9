Our Story

Welcome to Furniture by Group9, a place where craftsmanship meets comfort. Established in 2024, we've been dedicated to providing stylish, durable, and sustainable furniture that transforms your house into a home. Our designs blend timeless elegance with modern functionality, ensuring every piece stands the test of time.

Quality & Materials

We believe in the quality of materials that go into each piece of furniture. From solid wood to eco-friendly finishes, our products are made with the finest materials, ensuring long-lasting durability and beauty.
